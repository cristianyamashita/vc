Camera + balloon physics

Vision Balloon Ball

A ping-pong-sized ball floats like a small air balloon. Hit it with your hand and it drifts away, bounces off walls and ceiling, then rests on the floor.

Open hand works best
Slow gravity
Screen walls bounce
  1. Start the camera and keep your hand inside the stage.
  2. Push through the ball with your palm or fingertips to send it away from your hand.
  3. The side walls and ceiling bounce it back; the floor catches it.
  4. Webcam access usually needs localhost or HTTPS in modern browsers.
